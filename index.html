Bank wants women in corporate leadership

The Chief Executive Officer, Premium Trust Bank, Emmanuel Emefienim, said the active participation of women in corporate leadership will help the country to achieve growth and sustainable development.
He disclosed this on March 8, during a landmark celebration of International Women’s Day, with the theme, ‘Inspire her: Inspiring women for growth and inclusion,’ in Lagos State.

Emefienim stated that women had made significant contributions to corporate decisions and economic advancement across the country.

He emphasised that the novel titled, ‘She Premium,’ was launched at the event to serve as a creative solution designed just for women to assist in their personal and professional growth.

He said, “Women play a critical role in corporate decision-making and the broader economy. Women possess immense potential that, when harnessed in decision-making roles, can lead to unparalleled growth and innovation in the country, as is the case with PremiumTrust Bank.
